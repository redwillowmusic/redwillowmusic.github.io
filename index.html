The Studios

Red Willow Studio

817 Battlefield Dr, Nashville TN 37204

When your music calls for keyboards, Red Willow Studio has all the bases covered. The live room contains a blend of absorption and diffusion panels, with 2 large gobos on casters for flexible treatment. The iso-booth (4’ x 8’ x 8’) allows for dry tracking of vocals, winds, strings and percussion.

Keyboards:
  • 5’2” Boston baby grand piano
  • Korg Stage Vintage keyboard
  • 1958 Hammond M3 organ
  • Novation Ultranova DCO synthesizer
  • Industry-standard software synths (incl. Omnisphere, Massive, Absynth, Reaktor, Albion, Zebra)

The large, private back patio is nice for quiet breaks. You are welcome to use the newly renovated kitchen, gas grill, full guest bathroom, and multi-arcade machine!

Iron Oak Recording

4504 Meta Dr, Nashville TN 37204

Iron Oak Recording is a robust tracking studio, suitable for simultaneous band recording. Its musical offerings include:

  • Many boutique guitars and amplifiers
  • Diverse pallete of industry standard microphones (Miktek, Sennheiser, Shure, Electro-Voice, Audio Technica, Rodes, etc.)
  • Ludwig Classic and Tama Starclassic drum kits
  • Studio upright piano
  • UAD and API preamps
